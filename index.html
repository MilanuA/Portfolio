Hello there!

My name is Alex and I am a game developer.

I started playing games when I was about 10, shortly after getting my first laptop. Almost five years later, I started learning programming, and during high school I learned C#. It was around that time, when I started playing one particular game, which really stood out to me, and I spent hours and hours playing it. After I fisnihed it, I was left with so many feelings - both good and bad. As I was thinking about the game even more, I realized something — people all over the world might be feeling the same way as I was.

Thanks to that moment, I realized one thing: I want to work on games. Why? Because games bring people together, they create stories and characters that stick with people.

My dream is to work on a game that everyone enjoyes and, years later, look back on and say: "Oh, I remember this game! I used to play it back in the old good days!"

And here I am - on my journey of learning game development, with the goal of creating experiences that stay with people.
