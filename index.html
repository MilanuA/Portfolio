Hello there!

My name is Alex and I am a game developer.

I started playing games when I was about 10, shortly after getting my first laptop. Almost five years later, I started learning programming, and during high school I learned C#. Around that time, one particular game really stood out to me. After finishing it, I was left with so many emotions, and it hit me — people all over the world might be feeling the same way.

That was when I realized I wanted to create something that brings people together. A game that everyone can enjoy and, years later, look back on and say, "Oh, I remember this game! I used to play it back in the days!"

Now, I'm on my journey of learning game development, with the goal of creating experiences that stay with people.
